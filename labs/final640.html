Lab Final

Download the 640finaldataset.txt file. It is an underscore-delimited file which contains simulated data from 11 independent groups of participants (labeled "A" through "K") with 100 participants in each group. Write a script that does the following:

  1. Remove the data from group C
  2. Print the grand mean of all observations
  3. Perform a series of two-tailed t-tests that compares the mean of each group to the mean of group A. You may use the t.test function, but it may only appear 1 time in your program
